Result

Dynamic spine values of the bow and of the selected arrows should match. A small deviation in the range of a few pounds from the optimal value, depending of personal preferences, will normally not affect the arrow flight. In doubt, slightly weak arrows should be chosen, i.e. with a higher spine value or fewer pounds. Experieced archers can tolerate stiffer arrows, however.
